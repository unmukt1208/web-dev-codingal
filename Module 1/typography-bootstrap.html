Football

is a sport where there are 11 players on each team.
The field is 105x68 meters.
There is a goal on each end and the Objective of each team is to score the most goals while being able to defend their goal.You score goals by using your feet to kick the ball. Everyone except the goalkeeper Can't use their hands.The strikers job is to be in the right positions to score. The wingers help give the ball to the striker. Midfielders help give key passes. Defenders hlep defend the goal and the goalkeeper is the final line of defence for the team.
